Beaches


With some of the most beautiful beach destinations in the world, Sri Lanka offers a range of landscapes, from rainforests to endless miles of powdery, white-sand beach.

Nilaveli Beach

Nilaveli Beach is a broad, sandy beach, lined with palm trees and dotted with fishermen's boats. It's a 30-minute drive from the city of Trincomalee. This tiny island has calm beaches with gentle surf due to its own small reef. There are lifeguards on duty and many little restaurants along the beach, most specializing in seafood.

Kalkudah Beach

A mile-long beach on Pasikuda Bay is perfect for swimming and wading. The water is shallow which will allow you to walk out into the ocean for hundreds of yards. The sand is bright white and soft, with fine grains, and the beach is lined with coconut trees. This area is just starting to be discovered so there is plenty of untouched beach to explore.

Bentota Beach

Bentota is a well known area only an hour away from Colombo. The sand is bright white on this wide beach, and the sea is bright aqua-blue due to its light-colored sandy bottom. The beach is surrounded by jungle and tall palm trees. Nearby attractions include many temples and remnants of the country's colonial past, like a fort protecting the old harbor of Galle.
