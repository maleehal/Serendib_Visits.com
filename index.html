Sri Lanka is an island located in the Indian Ocean. It is located between the Laccadive Seain west and the Bay of Bengal in east. Sri Lanka is only 2441km away from India and is divided from a semi-enclosed water body known as the Palk Bay. This beautiful island consists of a main land and a number of smaller islands. Sri Lanka was and is still called the Gem of the Indian Ocean For its great treasures. It was called by many names such as Taprobane, Serendib and Ceylon. Currently its well known as Sri Lanka.

Why Sri Lanka

Do you like:

  • golden beaches
  • misty mountains
  • mighty elephants
  • giant whales
  • striking past
  • tasteful tea
Then Sri Lanka is your paradise. In this precious island you can experience both cold and warm weather. With many sites and scenes bottled up in to a small island, a traveler could be riding the waves in the dawn and admiring the green carpeted mountains by dusk. The smiles and hospitality of Sri Lanka are world famous and so are its spicy food, exotic fruits, and an array of sweetmeats found nowhere in the world. With so many cultures living next to each other life in Sri Lanka continues among a series of festivities throughout the year, an ideal recipe for fun and leisure.
