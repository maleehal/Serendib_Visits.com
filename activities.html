Activities


You can find plebty of thrilling, adventourous and exciting activities in Sri Lanka. From diving in the deepest rivers and ocean to climbing the highest mountains and rocks the fun will never end. Apart from natural adventourous step into some jerky rides at the amusement parks. No matter your vacation is 1 week, 3 weeks or 1 month we got you covered with these amazing activities.

Rock Climbing

If you want to feel the wind hit your body while hanging from a rope then this is your call. Sri Lanka has mountains such as Knuckles, Ella, Habarana, etc... followed by rocks such as Sigiriya. All these places provide suitable climbing spots for both armature and experienced climbers.

Diving

Being surrounded by the indian ocean with coral, exotic fish and intrestind wrecks, Sri Lanka offers unique diving experiences to an individual. Galle harbore and little and great basses offers great shipwreck sites. skin diving and diving with dolphins can be done at Kalpitiya, Ampara and trincomalee.

Hot Air Ballooning

Enjoy the skies with a hot air balloon ride. This will be a relaxing experience by floating 500 to 2000 feet on average. This effortless drift over lakes, rivers and tree tops will give you a perfect view of Sri Lanka. Target your ride on sunrise or sunset for the best experience.
